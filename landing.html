Frequently asked questions

Do I need internet to use it?
Only for the first setup and to activate your license. After that, Sessions Garden works fully offline.
Can I use it on multiple devices?
Your license includes 2 device activations. Note that data is stored locally and does not sync between devices.
Is my clients' data safe?
Yes. All data is stored only on your device. Nothing is sent to any server. The development team has zero access to your information.
What healing methods does it support?
Designed for energy healing practices, it tracks your sessions. Track released emotions, physical imbalances, limiting beliefs, and more. This tool can support many methods.
Can I still write on paper?
Absolutely. Many practitioners write during sessions and enter the summary into Sessions Garden afterwards. It's designed to fit your workflow, not replace it.
What if I switch devices?
You can activate your license on a new device. Remember to export your data from the old device first, as data is stored locally.
Is there a subscription?
No. One payment, lifetime access. No monthly fees, ever.
What if my computer breaks or gets lost?
Since all data is stored locally, it's important to regularly export your data using the built-in export function. This creates a backup file you can save anywhere safe (USB drive, email to yourself, etc.). Sessions Garden does not store your data in the cloud — which means full privacy, but also means you are responsible for keeping backups.
Can I restore my data on a new device?
Yes. Import your exported backup file into Sessions Garden on your new device and your sessions and clients will be restored.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes. If the tool doesn't fit your needs, contact us within 14 days of purchase and you'll receive a full refund.

Right to access: your data is already on your device — open the app to see it. Right to deletion: clear your browser's site data to remove all stored information. Right to data portability: use the Export function in the app to download your data as a JSON file.
