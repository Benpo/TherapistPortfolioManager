# Brand Voice Research — Sessions Garden

**Source:** Interview with Sapir, 2026-03-13
**Status:** Raw insights for landing page copy

---

## Origin Story
Sapir learned an energy healing method and received a digital tool during studies. When studies ended, the tool was taken away. She went back to paper — a drawer stuffed with pages, hard to find past sessions, hard to remember treatment sequences, hard to prepare for next sessions. Then moved to computer files but lacked structure. Built Sessions Garden as an upgraded version of the study tool — her "dream tool."

## Core Emotions & Metaphors

**"The quiet corner"** — Sessions Garden is the calm space a practitioner returns to when she has self-doubt, to see the path she's walked and the good results she's had with clients.

**"The garden"** — Each client is a plant that needs different care. The practitioner tends her garden.

**"Stuffed drawer → digital order"** — The transformation from paper chaos to structured, searchable digital records.

## Target User
Energy healing practitioners (various methods) who:
- Currently use paper or unstructured files
- Treat multiple clients and need to track history
- Care about client confidentiality
- Want to prepare efficiently for sessions
- May have been using a method for years and are "stuck" in paper

## Pain Points (from Sapir's experience)
1. Drawer full of papers — takes time to find past treatments
2. Hard to remember treatment sequences and prepare for next session
3. Computer files lack structure and organization
4. Fear of exposing sensitive client information
5. Lost the study tool and had no replacement
6. Paper practitioners want to keep writing but need a way to summarize and document digitally

## What Makes It Different
- Built BY a practitioner, FOR practitioners
- Supports advanced techniques (physical imbalances, limiting beliefs, tools from multiple methods)
- Complete privacy — nothing leaves the device
- Multilingual (EN, HE, DE, CS)
- No subscription — pay once, own forever

## What The User Should Feel After 1 Week
- "Finally I have a tool that keeps structure, documentation, and order"
- "I don't need to scatter — I know exactly where to find everything"
- "It answers all my personal needs to treat in the best, most comfortable way"
- "I feel safe about my data and trust the system"

## Key Messaging Pillars
1. **Convenience** — your way, anywhere, anytime
2. **Innovation** — no more paper shuffling and searching for hours
3. **Privacy** — local data, confidential for practitioner and clients
4. **Simplicity** — one-time payment, no commitments

## IP Note
Do NOT use "Emotion Code" or "Body Code" (Dr. Nelson's IP). Use generic terms: "energy healing", "holistic practitioners", "energy-based therapies", "energetic methods".

## Brand Name Meaning
"Sessions Garden" = the practitioner's garden of sessions. Each client is a plant needing different care. A beautiful garden she nurtures. Hebrew resonance: "גינת הטיפולים שלי."
