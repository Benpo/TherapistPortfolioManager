## Sozialversicherung (Health Insurance) — Critical Threshold

Since Ben is employed 3 days/week, his employer handles Krankenversicherung. Sapir's side business is ignored for social insurance IF:

- Self-employment hours stay **≤20 hours/week**
- Self-employment income stays **below employment income** (~€78K)

**Since Sapir is the Gewerbetreibende (not Ben):** The analysis differs. If Sapir has no other employment, she may need her own health insurance arrangements depending on how she's currently insured (likely familienversichert through Ben's employer). Key considerations:

- **Familienversicherung threshold:** If Sapir's monthly Gewerbe income exceeds **€505/month** (2026, ~€6,060/year), she loses eligibility for Familienversicherung and must get her own Krankenversicherung

**This is a critical cliff to monitor** — at ~60 sales/year (€5,940), Sapir could lose Familienversicherung.

## The Nachzahlung + Vorauszahlung Double-Hit

1. **Nachzahlung** — back-taxes for that year

**Worst case (late filing, both hit at once):**

| Gewerbe Income | Back-tax | + Prepayments | **Total first bill** |

## EU Widerrufsrecht (14-Day Withdrawal)

Can be excluded for digital goods under **§356 Abs. 5 BGB** if ALL conditions met:
1. Consumer explicitly consents (unchecked checkbox) to immediate delivery
2. Consumer acknowledges loss of withdrawal right
3. Confirmation sent on durable medium (email)

**Required checkbox text:**
> [ ] Ich stimme der sofortigen Vertragsausfuehrung vor Ablauf der Widerrufsfrist zu und nehme zur Kenntnis, dass dadurch mein Widerrufsrecht erlischt.

**Lemon Squeezy:** Partially covers this in buyer terms but may not fully satisfy German §356 BGB requirements (no explicit checkout checkbox, no separate post-purchase confirmation, governed by English law). Verify with LS support or add supplementary notice.

**If NOT excluded:** Customer can use product for 14 days, withdraw, get full refund, owe nothing. Missing information entirely extends period to 12 months + 14 days. Abmahnung risk: €1,500-5,000+.
