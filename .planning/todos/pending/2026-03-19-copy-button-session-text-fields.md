## Problem

When reviewing a session, the therapist often wants to copy specific text field values (insights, comments, important points) to share with the client. Currently there's no quick way to do this — requires manual text selection.

## Solution

Add a small "copy" icon button next to each text field in the session form. Clicking it copies the field's content to clipboard with a brief toast confirmation.
