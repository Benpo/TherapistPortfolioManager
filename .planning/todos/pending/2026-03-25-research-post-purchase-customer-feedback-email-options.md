## Problem

After a customer purchases and activates Sessions Garden, there's no mechanism to collect feedback, request reviews, or gauge satisfaction. We want to reach out to customers post-purchase but need to understand what's legally allowed under German law (DSGVO, UWG) before implementing anything.

Key questions:
1. Can we email customers after purchase for feedback/satisfaction surveys? What's the legal basis?
2. What timing is appropriate (and legal) for post-purchase outreach?
3. Can we request reviews or testimonials?
4. Does UWG §7 (unzumutbare Belästigung) restrict post-purchase emails?
5. What does Lemon Squeezy provide — customer emails, webhooks, metadata?
6. Can LS's transactional email system be used for feedback requests, or does that violate their ToS?

## Solution

Research phase — check:
- DSGVO Art. 6(1)(f) (legitimate interest) as basis for customer satisfaction emails
- UWG §7 Abs. 3 (existing customer exception for email marketing)
- LS API capabilities for customer communication
- Best practices from German indie SaaS sellers
- Consider: in-app feedback prompt vs. email vs. both
- Low priority — defer to Phase 21+
