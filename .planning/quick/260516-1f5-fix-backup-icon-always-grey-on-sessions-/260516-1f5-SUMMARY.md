**One-liner:** Sessions and Reporting pages now load the `jszip + backup.js + backup-modal.js` trio after `app.js` (mirroring the 4 already-working pages), so the header backup cloud icon's color is derived from the real `portfolioLastExport` timestamp instead of being permanently grey — a CRITICAL production bug on the live app, fixed with zero production-logic changes and proven by a falsifiable RED→GREEN behavior test.
