## Adjudication: Session Format term (queued from Plan 06) — VERDICT: ACCEPTED

Plan 06 rendered the HE changelog's "Session Format" as **סוג/אופן המפגש** (not the shipped in-app filter label **אופן הטיפול**, Phase 37-11) because the shipped label contains **טיפול**, a clinical root the 42.1 HE forbidden-term gate rejects. As the native-register gate, the verdict is **the divergence is acceptable — in fact preferable**:
1. **Forced, not careless.** The shipped label אופן הטיפול cannot be carried verbatim into a 42.1-gated file without failing the forbidden-clinical-term gate.
2. **Out of scope to fix the shipped label.** אופן הטיפול is a **pre-v1.3** string (Phase 37-11); pre-Phase-39 i18n is explicitly OUT of 42.1's scope (NOT-list). The gate cannot rewrite it here.
3. **The paraphrase is better on the merits.** סוג המפגש ("session type") is register-consistent (uses מפגש, the corpus's session term) and semantically **more faithful** to the English "Session Format" than the shipped אופן הטיפול ("mode of treatment").
4. **Live-token note:** in the help body the filter is referenced via `{ui:filter.sessionFormat}`, which resolves at render time to the live shipped label (אופן הטיפול). So a HE reader may see אופן הטיפול in the help topic's live label yet סוג המפגש in the changelog prose. This is acceptable — descriptive prose paraphrasing a UI label is normal — but is **flagged for Plan 10 to surface in Sapir's read**, since the underlying shipped label אופן הטיפול itself carries a clinical root that a future (out-of-42.1) pass may want to retire for whole-app alignment.

**Recorded for Plan 10:** surface (a) the accepted סוג המפגש changelog paraphrase and (b) the observation that the shipped filter label אופן הטיפול still carries טיפול, as a candidate for a future non-42.1 cleanup.
