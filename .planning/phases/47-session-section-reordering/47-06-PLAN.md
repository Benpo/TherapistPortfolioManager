<objective>
Make the section order durable across the encrypted backup boundary and defensive against crafted/old backups. Purpose: ORDR-05 (persist + encrypted-backup round-trip) and Pitfall 2 (the clamp must apply on restore, not just in the drag handler).

Output: backup.js restore branch + allowlist updates; an encrypted round-trip + sanitization test.
</objective>
