**Step 1: Define DOODLE_ICONS constant in landing.js**

- Use `width="36" height="36" viewBox="0 0 36 36"`
- Use `fill="none" stroke="currentColor" stroke-linecap="round" stroke-linejoin="round"`
- Have organic, hand-drawn paths (slightly wobbly coordinates)
- Vary stroke-width: outlines ~1.5-2, details ~0.8-1.2

Six icons needed:
1. `folder` — Open folder with a small leaf or sprout growing from the top tab. Folder shape slightly irregular (not perfectly rectangular). Leaf has a tiny vein line.
2. `lock` — Simple padlock with a vine or tendril wrapping around the shackle. Lock body slightly rounded/organic. One small leaf on the vine.
3. `offline` — Laptop/device silhouette (simple rectangle with slight curve) with a small leaf or plant sprouting from the keyboard area. No wifi symbol needed.
4. `sparkle` — Small star/sparkle shape with botanical accent — a flower bloom with 4-5 petals radiating from center, with 2 small leaves below.
5. `coin` — Circle (coin) with a small leaf motif inside or growing from the edge. Circle slightly imperfect (hand-drawn).
6. `notebook` — Open notebook/book shape with a small flower or pen beside it. Pages slightly wavy to show they're open.
