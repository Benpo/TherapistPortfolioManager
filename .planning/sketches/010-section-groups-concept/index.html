Where does "Issue severity" live? (applies to either model)

1 · Free — drag it anywhere

Severity is an ordinary item with its own handle. Maximum freedom; its current position just becomes the default.

⠿Session topics
⠿Issue severity
⠿Emotions & Techniques
⠿Session Notes and Observations

2 · Glued — always follows Session topics

Severity has no handle; it auto-travels with Session topics (severity is an attribute of the topics). One invisible rule to learn.

⠿Session topics
🔗Issue severity
⠿Emotions & Techniques
⠿Session Notes and Observations

3 · Pinned — fixed near the end

Severity can't move at all; everything else reorders around it. Least code, least freedom — the one row that says "no".
