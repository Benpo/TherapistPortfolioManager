The technical bits, in plain language

Data never leaves your browser

Everything you record lives on this device only. Nothing is sent anywhere — that's the whole point. It also means you are the only backup.

Backing up & restoring

Export a backup file (optionally encrypted), and restore it on this or another device.

iPhone & iPad (Safari)

Open the Share menu, then choose Add to Home Screen. (Illustrated steps in the real page.)

Chrome & Edge

Look for the install icon at the end of the address bar.

Android

Open the browser menu and choose Install app.

Activating your license

Enter your license key once — it needs internet just for that moment.

Moving to a new computer

Your license covers two devices; deactivate before you transfer.
