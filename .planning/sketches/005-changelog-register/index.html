Version 1.2

July 2026

This update is all about making the app feel like your practice — your date format, your session formats, and faster ways to find the session you're looking for.

Dates, your way

  • NewA “Personalize” tab in Settings — choose how dates appear throughout the app, or leave it on Auto to follow your language.
  • ImprovedDates now look the same everywhere — client lists, session tables, and PDF exports all follow your chosen format.

Session formats, your way

  • Improved“Session Type” is now Session Format — the way you meet: in-person, online, remote, proxy, or other.
  • NewRename the built-in formats or add your own in Settings → Personalize.

Find sessions faster

  • NewA Session Format filter on the Overview and Sessions pages — pick one or several formats at once.
  • NewA Heart-Wall toggle — one switch to show only clients or sessions where a Heart-Wall was worked on.
  • NewClick the Name, Sessions, or Last Session column headers to sort; click again to flip the order.

Small but meaningful

  • ImprovedHeart-Wall is now named consistently across the whole app, in all four languages.
  • FixedUpdates now install reliably on Safari — no more stuck old versions.
  • FixedIf the database ever hiccups, the app can now recover itself without losing your work.

New

  • A “Personalize” tab in Settings — choose how dates appear throughout the app, or leave it on Auto to follow your language.
  • Rename the built-in session formats or add your own in Settings → Personalize.

Fixed
